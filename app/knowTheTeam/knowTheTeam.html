Team BiggIdeas

Meet the developers of this app! Arlin Grijalba and Stephen Rosso meet at Origin Code Academy where they formed the group BiggIdeas, and began sharing ideas of apps they wanted to create. With 10 years experience at Costco, Stephen and his team saw a need for an app to increase productivity and he shared this idea with Arlin. Arlin already having a computer science background and 4 years of experience she understood the project and was ready to get started on building Dropco.

"Arlin's thorough knowledge of C# and AngularJS was a huge key to our success." -Stephen Rosso
